Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

"No More Wasted Space" (Gridfinity Drawer Designer) is a free tool for designing custom Gridfinity drawer layouts. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All design work happens locally on your device.

Contact Information

When you submit a design, the app opens your device's email client with the design specification pre-filled. The contact information you optionally provide (name, email, phone, address) is included in that email which you send directly from your own email account. We do not have access to this information until you choose to send the email.

No Tracking

This app does not use analytics, cookies, advertising trackers, or any third-party tracking services.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share since we do not collect any.

Local Storage

The app may use your browser's local storage to cache the application for offline use (via a service worker). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
